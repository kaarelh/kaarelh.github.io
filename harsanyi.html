Becoming Harsanyier (work in progress)

Here’s a picture which probably fails to capture some important stuff, but I think is cool enough to be worth considering anyway. Maybe becoming a reasonable/stable person1 is about trading off consistently between one’s various subagents/values. fun-you not doing cocaine out of care for work-you or family-you, and so on

Suppose you realize that you have not been trading off consistently between two values in different kinds of situations

do convergence to harsanyi, trading well between parts, e.g. why should treat a good org’s money as your own

e.g. eating meat vs spending less and donating

There is a bad sense of this, or at least a sense in which this is a sign of something bad, for example when one reflects upon the last year and realizes ones has mostly not changed one’s (ethical) beliefs, which I would guess is often a result of suffering a generalized Erdősian death.
