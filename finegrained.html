Fine-grained complexity, an advertisement (work in progress)

In a recent icebreaker game, my prompt to tell my game partner about my coolest memory. Anyway, while then telling my interlocutor about one particularly cool result from fine-grained complexity, I realized that I had forgotten the details of its proof, which is not a fine state of affairs at all (though the forgotten details were at a resolution too fine to significantly affect the conversation).
